About Us

The San Gregorio Magno Altar Servers are a dedicated group of young individuals who serve in the sacred ministry of assisting the priest during Mass and other liturgical ceremonies at our parish. This ministry is vital to the smooth running of our worship services, and our altar servers play an integral role in making each Mass a reverent and prayerful experience.
