About Us

The San Gregorio Magno Altar Servers are a dedicated group of young individuals who serve in the sacred ministry of assisting the priest during Mass and other liturgical ceremonies at our parish.

Our mission is to provide a sacred, dignified, and prayerful presence at the altar of God. We believe that through our service, we are not only helping the Church but also growing in faith, discipline, and commitment.

The altar servers at San Gregorio Magno represent the youth of our parish who are eager to serve and embody the values of respect, reverence, and humility.

Our ministry is open to all youth who are willing to take on the responsibility of assisting in the liturgical celebrations. We welcome both new and experienced servers.

If you feel called to serve, we encourage you to join our altar servers' ministry and help us bring the light of Christ to our parish through service and devotion.
