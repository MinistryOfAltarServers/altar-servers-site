About Us

The San Gregorio Magno Altar Servers are a dedicated group of young individuals who serve in the sacred ministry of assisting the priest during Mass and other liturgical ceremonies at our parish. This ministry is vital to the smooth running of our worship services, and our altar servers play an integral role in making each Mass a reverent and prayerful experience.

Our mission is to provide a sacred, dignified, and prayerful presence at the altar of God. We believe that through our service, we are not only helping the Church but also growing in faith, discipline, and commitment. Serving as an altar server is a way to deepen our relationship with Christ and become part of something larger than ourselves.

The altar servers at San Gregorio Magno represent the youth of our parish who are eager to serve and embody the values of respect, reverence, and humility. Whether itâ€™s assisting with the cross, candles, incense, or other sacred duties, every role is significant and valued. As altar servers, we are trained not only in the practical aspects of the role but also in the spiritual importance of our service to God.

Our ministry is open to all youth who are willing to take on the responsibility of assisting in the liturgical celebrations. We welcome both new and experienced servers, and our training sessions ensure that everyone feels confident and prepared in their role.

If you feel called to serve, we encourage you to join our altar servers' ministry and help us bring the light of Christ to our parish through service and devotion. Let us work together in prayerful unity to glorify God and serve His Church.
