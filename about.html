About Our Ministry

The San Gregorio Magno Altar Servers are dedicated youth who assist during Masses and liturgical celebrations. We strive to serve with reverence, responsibility, and joy, continuing the tradition of faithful altar service within our parish.

Our schedule, roles, and responsibilities are all available in the Schedule tab, and we are open to new members at any time!
